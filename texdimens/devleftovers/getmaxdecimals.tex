\input xintexpr.sty

% from jfbu's Nachlass :

\xintdeffunc Nmax(psi) := ceil(2**14 * psi) - 1 ;

\xintdeffunc Amax(psi) := 1e17 - 
             (2 * (2**16 * ceil(2**14 * psi) - ceil(2**30 * psi)) + 1) * 5**17
                          - 1 ;

% Jeudi 01 juillet 2021
% bug of xint 1.4i, can not use [Nmax(x), Amax(x)] in seq ?
% Ah ok, the balancing hiding the inner comma does not work with square
% brackets. Let's use ([Nmax(x), Amax(x)]) and it works to hide the inner comma
% And I did not need to use [...] to start with.
% I probably need to add this to bug/feature list though

\immediate\write128{\xinteval{seq(([Nmax(x), Amax(x)]), x = 
        1           ,
        800/803     ,
        642/685     ,
        1157/1238   ,
        2540/7227   ,
        1/12        ,
        107/1370    ,
        1157/14856  ,
        254/7227    ,
        100/7227    )}}

\bye

This gives maximal inputs with up to 17 decimal places
not triggering Dimension too large.

Alternatively, replacing the ending 4 by 5 this gives
minimal decimals triggering Dimension too large.
